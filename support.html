Common Questions

How do I create an account?
Download Crown & Caddie from the App Store and sign up with your email or Apple ID.

How do I list an item for sale?
Go to the Marketplace tab, tap the sell button, and follow the prompts to add photos, description, and price.

How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account, or email us at support@crownandcaddie.com.

Is my payment information secure?
Yes. All payments are processed through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. We never store your full card details.
